Mr. Robot

Mr. Robot is an American drama–thriller television series created by
Sam Esmail

Mr. Robot follows Elliot, a young programmer working as a cyber-security engineer by day, and a vigilante hacker by night.

Elliot Alderson is portrayed by Rami Malek
